About

Hi There!

In 1961, my grandpa, Carl Sr., created "Carl's Car Show." When my grandpa retired in 1998, we agreed that the car show would only accept cars 25 years old and older. Since then, I have grown the car show to be the biggest vintage car show in America. We had 1,008 cars register last year, and we hope to have even more this year. I'm excited to see you at the show!

50/50 Raffle!

There will be volunteers going around the show selling 50/50 raffle tickets for $10 each. At the end of the show, we will pick one winner who will win this beautiful 1961 vintage car and 50% of the money collected. The other 50% of money will be donated to Cars for Veterans which is a great organization that helps veterans afford transportation.
